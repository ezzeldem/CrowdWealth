Overview

Weelo Business is a cloud-based, B2B2C, multi-sided platform that is digitizing the FMCG Supply Chain in Egypt, from distribution warehouse management to cash collection. Our platform empowers suppliers, distributors and customers and enables new and interactive access to new customers, orders, inventory and data management in just a few clicks.

Problem

X Due to the lack of appropriate systems, there is an acute and cost inefficiency amongst retailers in Egypt, along with most of the multinational and local brands.

Solution

X Weelo provides an AI-powered omniplatform that enables SME’s of different functions to connect, communicate, and collaborate in more scalable and efficient ways along the supply chain. The platform optimizes and synchronises operations and gives businesses the ability to boost their efficiency and productivity to compete with industry players. Weelo is the only omniplatform solution that integrates both key accounts and traditional trade and connects them to retailers directly.

Impact

By providing every manufacturer, distributor, supplier and retailer with an affordable digital interface for their operations, Weelo aims to accelerate industry growth while empowering and digitizing every participant and of the supply chain process.

Business model

X Weelo offers packages starting from $150/month for up to 3 users (catered more to small enterprises), to a $350/month pro package that holds up to 15 users and the platform can also be customized for Enterprise packages that range from $450-$1500/month. Being multi-sided, Weelo also innovates by incorporating the B2B2C model and covers all bases of the FMCG supply chain. Data on consumer/buyer behavior, consumption trends in industry along with hundreds of other data points can be collectively aggregated and offered to market analysis agencies.

Competition

Traditional Trade solutions are offered by competitors like SalesForce and SAP. There are no currently available other comprehensive platforms like WEELO’s. WEELO is currently the only omniplatform that integrates into Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems like Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, SAP and quickbooks to ensure seamless operations in any company. All direct competitors offer either components (ERP or Field Sales platform) with much more limited features than WEELO, with less competitive prices and package deals.
